Organ Donation Project

We Are

A community who seeks to raise awareness for organ donation through artwork and other mediums. Registering to become a potential organ donor when you die may be the simplest and most generous thing you ever do. By leaving your organs behind, you may save as many as eight (8 lives). What could be more generous than to give a fellow Canadian a chance to celebrate another birthday, graduate high school, travel the world, get married and have children?

Poster Campaign

Anyone who is an organ donar can submit artwork that can be viewed by the public eye, whether it be on the website or on a billboard

Community Events

The Organ Donation Project is about community involvment and bringing people together. We run and are involved in a number of events to help raise awareness and drive donor registration.
