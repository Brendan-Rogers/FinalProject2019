Organ Donation Project

We Are

A community who seeks to raise awareness for organ donation through artwork and other mediums. Registering to become a potential organ donor when you die may be the simplest and most generous thing you ever do. By leaving your organs behind, you may save as many as eight (8 lives). What could be more generous than to give a fellow Canadian a chance.
