Michael Gibson Exhibit

Our touring art show showcases the best and brightest in organ themed art exhibits in Ontario! This interactive, artist, educational event is guaranteed to have something for everyone. Featuring posters designed my people around the province, installation art, and a massive glass heart installation by the world famous sculptor Christine Lopez.

The show starts with a 2 week residence in the Micheal Gibson Gallery in London Ontario, between April 1st and April 14th, 2019. From there, we're heading to art galleries all around Ontario. Contact christine@organdonationproject.ca for more information.

Twitch Livestream

Twitch.com/OrganDonationProject is the place to be! Tune in on April 20th at 5 o'clock PM for an action-packed 24 hour livestream, featuring young talent from around the province drawing fresh Organ posters live on air. If you bring your Twitch.tv account, you can even interact with the artists in real time!

It costs only $5 to become a "subscriber" to the channel! Earn a live shout-out, unlock custom "emotes" for use all across Twitch, and most importantly, support the worthy cause of raising awareness for organ donation in Ontario.

ODP Mobile

The ODP is on the go! Find us in malls, parks, and public spaces all across Ontario between April 1st and June 31st, 2019. Stop by to check out some of our most beloved feature posters, and grab some ODP swag while you're at it.

Pick up some free exclusive ODP stickers, pins, pens, and patches. Also feel free to check out our premium ODP Shirts and Beanies, selling for $20 each. All funds raised go to the continued promotion and research of organ donation.
